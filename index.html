Promoting Mental Health, in the Digital Age.

In today's digital age, mental health is of paramount importance. The constant exposure to the screens, social media and online interactions can lead to stress and anxiety.It's crucial to promote mental well-being to maintain a healthy and balanced life in our digitally connected world.
Discover resources and tools to help you navigate the challenges of the digital world while maintaining your mental well-being.

About Us

We take care of your mental well-being.

We at OASIS aim to build a mentally healthy society by attempting to encourage and increase protective factors and healthy behaviors that can help prevent the onset of a diagnosable mental disorder and reduce risk factors that can lead to the development of a mental disorder.

We address important issues like information overload, social media pressure online-harassment and promote awareness especially among the YOUTH and provide necessary aid to the victims of mental-illness, in order to build a mentally healthy society.
